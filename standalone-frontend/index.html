Our Campus

Velammal College of Engineering and Technology (VCET) is situated on the outskirts of Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India. The campus is 25 acres in size and is intended to provide a favourable learning environment for students. The campus has modern facilities such as well-equipped classrooms, labs, seminar halls, auditorium, library, sports facilities, and student dormitory lodging. The college also provides a cafeteria where students and staff may eat healthy and sanitary cuisine. The campus is tranquil and green, with planted gardens and open spaces, offering a peaceful environment for learning. The college has also placed a significant emphasis on sustainability, using eco-friendly practices such as rainwater collection, solar electricity, and garbage management. Overall, VCET offers a contemporary and well-equipped campus with an emphasis on sustainability and a calm learning environment.

Our Facilities

Library

The VCET library is housed in a large facility with modern amenities. It has a large collection of books, periodicals, journals, and reference materials spanning a wide range of subjects. The library has national and international journal and database subscriptions. Experienced librarians run the library and are constantly accessible to help students and educators.

Play Ground

VCET features a playground where students can participate in a variety of sporting activities. Cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, and other games are available on the playground. It is well-kept and provides a safe setting for pupils to improve their physical health and sporting abilities.

Auditorium

VCET has a modern and well-equipped auditorium with a seating capacity of around 1000 people. It is equipped with the latest audio-visual facilities, making it suitable for conducting seminars, conferences, and other events. The auditorium provides a comfortable and spacious environment for conducting various academic and cultural activities.
